About BatModel3D

Our Story

Founded in 2020, BatModel3D emerged from a collaboration between professional baseball equipment manufacturers and 3D modeling experts. Our mission is to provide the highest quality 3D models of baseball bats for manufacturers, designers, and sports enthusiasts.

Precision Models

High-accuracy 3D models with detailed measurements and specifications.

Multiple Formats

Custom Solutions

Tailored modeling services for specific manufacturing needs.
